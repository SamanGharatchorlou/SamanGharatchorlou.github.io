About

I make games in my free time using the Unity gaming engine. Everything on this website I have built myself (including the website). All of the games are totally free to play and you can find the source codes in the'Source Code' tab. Feel free to copy and use any of it.

All the games are in the alpha stage as I'm still working on them, updating them as time goes on. If you click on the 'More about this game' link sometimes I cover problems I faced and/or improvement recommendations and I'd love to hear from you.

Anyway enough chat, go checkout my games!

Aim

Stop the creeps from reaching your base! Creeps will follow the path around the map making their way to your base, where they are trying to steal your stuff, sneaky creeps. Build towers on the tiles to shoot them down before they reach the end. Warning its quite hard, so be prepared.

Developing This Game

This was my first big project, it was a challenge not just building the core game itself but also creating computer players (refered to AI, even though its not really AI). For the AI I had to build a fairly complex string of recursive functions (a function that calls itself), which was quite a challenge, particularly for the attack phase. The AI works on a set of basic rules that it follows giving the impression it knows (well sort of) what its doing.

Further Developments

I 'completed' this game about a month before I actually completed it becuase I was constantly adding little features here and there. There's a tonne of stuff I still want to add but dont have time to at the moment, a few of them below:

  • Graphics
    The game currently consists of boxes fighting eachother, which is kind of dull. I want to add actual soldiers with fighting, killing and dying animations to give the game more life.
  • Improved Computer Player
    As mentioned the current AI works on a few basic rules. I'm hoping to make the AI a little more situational so it can make some more complex decisions being more unpredictable.
  • Prediction Algorithm
    To do the above I need to build a prediction algorithm to calculate win/loss probabilities of certain battle situations and I'd like to add something into the game giving the player the abillity to calculate the chance of winning a fight, maybe once a turn or trade a soldier for 10 uses etc. I figured out equations to calculate 1v1, 1v2, 2v1 and 2v2 battles already. I havent been able to calculate the probability for 3v2 battle yet, I know what probabilities are but not the equation (not that you need it once you have the probabilities). If you know how to do it, let me know.
  • Dice Roll
    Ross wants to see what he has rolled so I'll add some dice animations.

There are more things like a death lazer, but i'll stop here before I get too carried away.
